About Us

Uncle Bing Bing is a YES business developed by students in Kingsway, selling a korean snowflake dessert known as Bingsu. The main reason we have decided to make this product is because it was rarely exposed in the area we live in. Local markets did not have Bingsu and it was perfect for our business as we can remotely do our sales, inside and outside of our school. We strive to make new innovative flavours as we progress and serve our delicious bowls of Bingsu to bring our customers a delightful experience of Korean culture. Check out our instagram or facebook page for frequent updates on events and sales locations! Any inquries can be sent to our Communication Operator.
